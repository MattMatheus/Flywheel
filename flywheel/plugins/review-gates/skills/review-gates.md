# Review Gates

Use this guidance when reviewing implementation handoffs or QA verdicts.

## Engineering Handoff Review

- Acceptance criteria are addressed.
- Validation evidence is specific.
- QA focus areas are explicit.
- Open risks are named rather than implied.
- State transition recommendation matches the evidence.

## QA Verdict Review

- Verdict is explicit.
- Missing validation is treated as a blocking issue.
- Bugs or follow-up work are filed when needed.
- Done-state promotion has supporting evidence.
